Overview

A comprehensive brand and marketing project spanning identity design, web, email, and advertising. From the ground up, the work established a cohesive visual language that carried consistently across every touchpoint.

The identity system formed the foundation — logo, typography, color, and guidelines — which then informed a full website, a suite of email templates, and a range of digital and print marketing materials.

The result was a brand that felt unified and intentional whether a customer encountered it on a billboard, in their inbox, or on a product page.
