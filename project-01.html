Brand Identity System

Type Branding
Scope Full Identity
Overview

A complete visual identity developed for an NYC venue management company to develop a brand identity that highlighted its female-founded, quirky, and exclusive character. Working closely with stakeholders, I coordinated the creation of custom illustrations and visual elements that authentically represented the brand's personality. I then extended this identity into a clean, intuitive web experience, ensuring consistency across digital touchpoints and strengthening the company's positioning in a competitive market.
