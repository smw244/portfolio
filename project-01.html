Brand Identity System

Type Branding
Scope Full Identity
Overview

A complete visual identity developed for a contemporary lifestyle brand entering a competitive market. The challenge was to craft something immediately recognizable while carrying enough depth to grow with the company over time.

The identity system spans logo, typography, color palette, iconography, packaging, and digital applications — built with strict modularity so it scales from a business card to a billboard without losing integrity.

The wordmark is set in a custom-modified serif, its letterforms subtly adjusted to introduce tension between old-world refinement and modern restraint. Every element in the system earns its place.
