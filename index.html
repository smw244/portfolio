About

Design with intention, illustration with instinct.

I'm Maren, an independent designer and illustrator based in New York. My practice spans brand identity, editorial illustration, and art direction — always rooted in a curiosity about how form, line, and colour can carry meaning.

With over eight years in the industry, I've collaborated with publishers, studios, and independent founders to create visual languages that feel both considered and alive.
